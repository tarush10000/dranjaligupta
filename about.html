About Us

Personal care for your healthy family

Dr. Anjali Gupta is a dedicated obstetrician-gynecologist with a deep commitment to women's health. She holds an MBBS and DNB in Obstetrics & Gynecology and is an ex-CMO of CGHS Meerut. Dr. Gupta is also a member of FOGSI, NARCHI, AOGD, and IMA, with a specialization in open and laparoscopic gynecological surgeries. Her years of experience and compassionate care have made her a trusted provider in the Meerut community.
